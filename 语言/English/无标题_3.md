「**would**」 + 「**if**」 + 「past simple」は仮定の状況を述べるのに使います。 🤔
例をいくつか見てみましょう！

| What **would** you do **if** we changed the plans? |
| --- |
| **Would** you be angry **if** I decided to stop the project? |
| How **would** you feel **if** I couldn't complete it before the deadline? |
