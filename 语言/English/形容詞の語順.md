> Adjectives follow a specific order: opinion, size, age, shape, colour, origin, material and purpose.

| category | Example         |
|----------|-----------------|
| opinion  | expensive shoes |
| size     | small shoes     |
| shape    | flat shoes      |
| age      | new shoes       |
| colour   | brown shoes     |
| origin   | Italian shoes   |
| material | leather shoes   |
| purpose  | walking shoes   |
